# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its academic purpose.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and direct collaboration channels related to the repository.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through a repository issue labeled `conduct`.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
